More Info

I made this app because I would often not notice my chat messages on my other monitor, and I'd prefer to have them come up on the display I'm looking at, rather than only in an OBS overlay.

Single Monitor Support

Stream Overlay is great for single monitor streamers, or streamers on the go without their usual setup.

Video and Audio

Based on Chromium, Stream Overlay has full support for video, audio, and animations in overlays.

Any Web Page

Just like an OBS overlay, Stream Overlay windows are browser windows and can show any web page.

Automations

Overlays can have automations, like TTS, emote walls, and video triggers, played right on your screen.
